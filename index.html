With ones you CARE, SHARE what you care

In hectic and difficult times, it’s easy to get caught engaging in activities you don’t want to do, or struggling to find time for the activities that you do want to do. We wish to provide a platform for people to track and share their hobbies and habits in order to motivate them to continue doing them and help keep friends accountable.
